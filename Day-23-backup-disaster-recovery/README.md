## Backup
- Regular data copies
- Protects against accidental deletion
- Example: EBS snapshots, RDS automated backups

## Disaster Recovery
- Recovery after major failure
- Minimizes downtime
- Types: Backup & Restore, Pilot Light, Warm Standby, Multi-site

## Business Continuity
- Ensures business operations continue
- Focuses on availability and recovery planning

## RTO (Recovery Time Objective)
- Maximum acceptable downtime
- Lower RTO = faster recovery

## RPO (Recovery Point Objective)
- Maximum acceptable data loss
- Lower RPO = more frequent backups

## AWS Backup Solutions
- EBS snapshots
- RDS automated backups
- Cross-region replication

## Key Takeaways
- DR planning reduces business risk
- RTO and RPO guide recovery strategy
